One Of A Kind Mixed Media Assemblages

by S. E. Cayleff

Cayleff's studio, WILD THINGS, encapsulates the natural and animal beauty central in her designs, emphasizing the profound connection between nature and humanity through many mediums–assemblages, mobiles, and paintings.

Explore Available Art Pieces

Pieces from the Gallery

In her creative process, Cayleff gathers relics from both the natural world and human history, highlighting how these objects preserve our memories and create stories. She culls the woods, beaches, mountains, deserts, and streets scavenging nature’s gifts: naturally-shed antlers, feathers, wood, bones, glass shards, rusted metal, and stunning ocean wash-ups. No creature was disturbed or interrupted during its life by the creation of this art. For a full read of our statement, visit our About section.
